Half-Life LAN Party 2025

Saturday, December 13 · Black Mesa Community Center · Doors open 9:00 AM

Schedule

  • Check in at 9:00 AM to claim your squad table.
  • Warm-up free play runs 9:30 AM to 10:30 AM.
  • Round-robin group matches kick off at 10:45 AM.
  • Top 8 single-elimination bracket starts at 3:30 PM.
  • Championship showdown launches at 6:00 PM sharp.

Registration & Gear

  • Register by December 1 to lock your rig space.
  • Bring your Windows PC, monitor (max 27"), and headset.
  • Pay the $45 entry fee online to confirm your slot.
  • Pack Ethernet cables and power strips for fast setup.

Tournament Rules

  • Balanced 4v4 team deathmatch using standardized loadouts.
  • Each round is first to 75 points or 15 minutes, whichever hits first.
  • Ties rerun on Sudden Death with a three-minute timer.
  • Scoreboard rankings award 3 points for a win, 1 for overtime loss.

Safety & Conduct

  • Check your gear through safety inspection before plugging in.
  • Respect retro hardware; no liquids on the floor or tables.
  • Follow staff direction on breaks and emergency exits.
  • Report any technical issues to logistics@halflifelanparty.com.
